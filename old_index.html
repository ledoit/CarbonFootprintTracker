1. Use Energy-Efficient Modes of Transportation

Finding energy-efficient modes of transportation such as walking, biking and public transportation can help reduce electricity consumption and lower your carbon footprint.

2. Carpool

Carpooling with friends is an energy-efficient way of lowering both of your carbon footprints. Carpooling can save up to 2,000 pounds (1 ton) of CO2 annually

3. Buy Hybrid or Eletric Veichles

When using personal vehicles, consider hybrid or electric cars for a greener option. These vehicles prdouce on average 120 grams less CO2 then their gasoline counterparts.

4. Leave During Optimal Traffic

Leaving early to avoid a route that consistently has traffic can save both time and CO2 from when you would be sitting in traffic
