Reduce Your Carbon Emissions

Calculator | History

1. Use Energy-Efficient Appliances

Choose appliances with the Energy Star label to save energy and reduce your carbon footprint.

2. Opt for Sustainable Transportation

Use public transportation, carpool, bike, or walk to reduce emissions from personal vehicles.

3. Conserve Water

Fix leaks, turn off taps when not in use, and consider water-saving appliances to conserve this precious resource.

4. Reduce, Reuse, Recycle

Minimize waste by following the three Rs. Reduce consumption, reuse items, and recycle materials whenever possible.

5. Go Plant-Based

Consider incorporating more plant-based meals into your diet to reduce the environmental impact of meat production.
